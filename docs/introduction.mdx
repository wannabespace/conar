Tamery is an AI-powered database client designed to make working with relational and analytical databases faster, smarter, and less frustrating. Whether you're a backend engineer inspecting production data, a data analyst running ad-hoc queries, or a team lead reviewing a schema with colleagues, Tamery gives you a unified interface to connect, explore, and query your databases — with an AI assistant that understands your schema and helps you get things done without leaving the app.

<Note>
  Tamery is currently in beta. Features and interfaces may change as the product evolves. Your feedback helps shape what comes next.
</Note>

## Supported Databases

Tamery currently supports the following database engines:

- **PostgreSQL** — full support for queries, browsing, schema inspection, and AI assistance
- **MySQL** — full support for queries, browsing, schema inspection, and AI assistance
- **MSSQL** (Microsoft SQL Server) — full support for queries, browsing, schema inspection, and AI assistance
- **ClickHouse** — full support for queries, browsing, schema inspection, and AI assistance
- **SQLite** — coming soon
- **MongoDB** — coming soon

## Available Platforms

Tamery is available in three forms so you can use it in whatever environment suits your workflow best.

**Web App** — Access Tamery directly at [tamery](https://tamery.app)[.app](https://conar.app) from any modern browser. No installation required. Sign in with your email or OAuth provider and connect to your databases right away.

**Desktop App** — Download the native desktop application for macOS, Windows, or Linux from [tamery](https://tamery.app/download)[.app/download](https://conar.app/download). The desktop app runs a local proxy on your machine, making it easy to connect to databases on localhost or within a private network.

**CLI** — Install the `tamery` command-line tool via npm for terminal-based access, scripting, and integration with your development workflow. The CLI requires Node.js version 20 or higher. Run `tamery login` to authenticate and `tamery proxy` to start a local proxy session.

## Security

Your database credentials are treated with care. Tamery encrypts all connection strings at rest, so your passwords and hostnames are never stored in plain text. You can choose how your connections are synced:

- **Cloud sync** — your encrypted connection details are stored in Tamery's cloud, making them available across all your devices when you sign in.
- **Local sync** — your connection details stay on your current device only, never leaving your machine.

In both modes, sensitive values like passwords are encrypted before storage. You are always in control of what gets saved and where.
