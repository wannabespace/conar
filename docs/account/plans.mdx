Tamery offers two plans designed to fit different needs. The **Free tier** gives you full access to Tamery's core database tooling — everything you need to browse, query, and visualize your data. When you're ready to bring AI deeper into your workflow, upgrading to the **Pro subscription** unlocks unlimited AI chat, SQL generation and editing, data seeding, and the ability to choose your preferred AI model.

<Note>
  Tamery is currently in beta. Pricing and plan features are subject to change as the product evolves. Check [tamery.app](https://tamery.app) for the most up-to-date pricing information.
</Note>

## Free Tier

The Free tier gives you immediate access to every core database feature in Tamery — no credit card required. You can connect to as many databases as you need and start working right away.

**What's included:**

- **Table Browser** — explore your tables, rows, and columns with a clean, intuitive interface
- **SQL Editor** — write and run queries directly against your database
- **Schema Visualizer** — understand the structure of your database and the relationships between tables at a glance
- **Definitions** — inspect table definitions, column types, indexes, and constraints
- **Unlimited connections** — connect to as many local or remote databases as you like
- **Cloud and local sync** — your connections are synced so they're available wherever you use Tamery
- **AI natural language filters** — filter table data using plain English queries, up to your monthly quota

The AI filter quota resets each month. If you find yourself hitting the limit, upgrading to Pro gives you unrestricted access.

## Subscription (Pro)

The Pro subscription builds on everything in the Free tier and adds the full suite of AI-powered features. If you work with databases regularly, these tools can dramatically speed up your workflow — from writing and fixing queries to seeding realistic test data.

**Everything in Free, plus:**

- **Unlimited AI chat assistant** — ask questions about your data, get query suggestions, debug problems, and explore your schema through a conversational interface in the Query view
- **AI SQL generation and editing** — describe what you want in plain language and let Tamery write or modify the SQL for you
- **Fix SQL with AI** — when a query returns an error, ask AI to diagnose and correct it automatically
- **Enhance Prompt** — refine and improve your natural language prompts so the AI produces better, more precise results
- **AI Data Seeding** — generate realistic, contextually appropriate seed data for your tables without writing fixtures by hand
- **Choose your AI model** — select the model that best suits your task from options including Claude Opus, GPT, Gemini, and more
- **Unlimited AI natural language filters** — use plain English to filter table data as often as you need, with no monthly cap

## Upgrading

Upgrading to Pro takes just a moment and is handled through a secure Stripe checkout.

1. Go to your account page at [tamery.app/account](https://tamery.app/account)
2. Click **Upgrade** under your current plan
3. Complete the checkout flow via Stripe

Your Pro features are activated immediately after your payment is confirmed. You'll receive a receipt from Stripe at the email address associated with your Tamery account.

<Tip>
  Visit [tamery.app](https://tamery.app) to see current pricing before you upgrade.
</Tip>

## Managing Your Subscription

All billing management is handled through the Stripe customer portal, accessible directly from your Tamery account page.

**From [tamery.app/account](https://tamery.app/account) you can:**

- **View your current plan** and renewal date
- **Access the billing portal** to update your payment method or billing details
- **Download invoices** for any past payments
- **Cancel your subscription** — your Pro access remains active through the end of your current billing period, after which your account reverts to the Free tier

To open the billing portal, go to [tamery.app/account](https://tamery.app/account) and click **Manage Billing**. You'll be taken to a Stripe-hosted page where all payment and subscription options are available.
